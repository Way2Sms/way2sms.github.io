    Way2SMS is a free application for Android and Java phones which allows you to send free SMS texts in India.

    Way2SMS is very simple and easy to use and allows users to send free SMS to any mobile network in India. In addition, it includes free email alerts and allows you to chat via your Gmail and Yahoo! accounts. Way2SMS works like any other messaging client - just create an account, log-on, and your ready to go.
